# Tempo Assistant

**Operator:** [tempo.co](https://tempo.co) | **Type:** Non-detector | **Cost:** Grant-funded

!!! abstract "TL;DR"
    An adjacent pointer, not a verification tool. Tempo's
    JournalismAI-prototype conversational interface that
    centralises five decades of editorial archive for an
    Indonesian newsroom; included here because newsroom AI
    orchestration is a coherent workflow design pattern, not
    because Tempo Assistant itself is something other newsrooms
    can deploy.

## What it does

Tempo Assistant is an internal Tempo prototype that lets
newsroom staff query roughly fifty years of Tempo's editorial
archive through a conversational interface. The tool surfaces
prior Tempo coverage relevant to a current verification or
investigative question; it does not detect manipulation, score
suspect content, or generate verdicts on specific claims.

## When to use it

A regional newsroom is studying how an Indonesian peer has
orchestrated AI assistance around verification work and wants a
documented design pattern to reference. A funder or institutional
partner is reviewing newsroom-AI prototypes in Southeast Asia
and Tempo's JournalismAI prototype is one of the relevant
SEA-grounded examples.

## Limitations

!!! info "Limitations"
    - Archival retrieval only; no real-time manipulation
    detection. Treating this as a detection
    or verification tool is a category error.
    - Internal Tempo prototype; not packaged for deployment by
    other newsrooms. The tool is documented through
    JournalismAI / IFCN ENGAGE channels rather than published
    as a reusable product.

## Privacy and threat model

Internal Tempo deployment runs on Tempo's editorial
infrastructure under that organisation's data-handling terms.
Other newsrooms that wished to build something similar would
inherit their own infrastructure decisions and would not
operate inside Tempo's data flow.

## Country and platform applicability

Indonesia only. The tool exists inside Tempo's
operational environment and is documented through Tempo's
JournalismAI prototype context.

## How to access

Internal Tempo deployment. External access is through
JournalismAI / IFCN ENGAGE prototype documentation; there is
no install or sign-up path for non-Tempo users.

## Cost (current as of 2026-05)

Grant-funded prototype inside Tempo. No external cost framework
because no external deployment surface exists.
